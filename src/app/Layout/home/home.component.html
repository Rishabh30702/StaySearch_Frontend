Prayagraj Maha Kumbh Mela 2025

Prayagraj holds a sacred significance as the confluence point of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers, making it one of the pivotal destinations during the upcoming Maha Kumbh Mela in 2025. This historic city in the state of Uttar Pradesh is a veritable treasure trove for Hindu pilgrims and history enthusiasts alike. It offers a rich tapestry of ancient temples, monuments, and various tourist attractions. At the heart of Prayagraj lies the revered Triveni Sangam, where the three holy rivers meet, an absolute must-visit for anyone attending the Maha Kumbh Mela in 2025.
